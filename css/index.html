How to Play

Objective: Predict how others rank personality traits about themselves.

Game Flow:

  1. One player is the "subject", others are "informants".
  2. 5 trait cards are drawn randomly.
  3. The subject ranks traits from "most like me" to "least like me".
  4. Informants predict the subject's ranking.
  5. Points are scored based on prediction accuracy.

Game Modes:

Classical: All players vs. the game.

Competitive: Players compete individually.
